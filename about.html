about me

I’m Karin. I live in Seattle with two cats and this guy.

Currently, I’m: a student at Ada Developer’s Academy, a lover of cats, and a proficient rock climber. I love making things, whether they're tangible craft projects, intangible tech projects, or delicious foods.
