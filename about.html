Hello! My name is Noelle Morris, and I am currently learning full-stack web development at Ada Developers Academy, and am a member of Cohort 6. I have been learning Ruby and HTML/CSS, and will soon begin learning Rails and JavaScript. I have also completed Udacity‘s Intro to Programming Nanodegree, which focuses on Python, HTML/CSS, and some JavaScript. I am not sure which programming language I like best—each has its own challenges. I am enjoying each new challenge that comes my way!

Before entering software, I wanted to work in archaeology, linguistics, or cultural anthropology. To that end, I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Anthropology (with a minor in Linguistics) from the University of Nevada, Reno. During the summer of 2012, I interned with the US Forest Service, doing archaeological survey work and excavation in Klamath National Forest in northern California.

In my spare time, I love reading, camping, hiking, backpacking, reading, cooking, and eating. Recently, I have gotten into sea kayaking.
