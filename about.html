Dianne Laguerta

Hello. My name is Dianne. Here's more about me.

I currently live in Seattle, studying at Ada Developers Academy since August 2016. At Ada, I'm studying to become a full-stack developer.

I'm originally from Jacksonville, Florida, and have lived in many places including along the East Coast and as far as Cairo, Egypt.

I graduated with a B.A. in History and Middle Eastern Studies from Mount Holyoke College, and have worked mostly in education and non-profit fields.

I have a love for music, zines, libraries, code, and beer! It's best to pair any of these things with beer.

Check out my projects or my blog to see my progression through the program!
