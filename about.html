Hi, I'm Heather and I do things with software!

Pittsburgh is home, but right now I live in Seattle, where I'm a Cohort 6 student at Ada Developers Academy. Ada focuses on Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, and CS Fundamentals, so those are my current areas of interest when it comes to computers. Once I get to my internship, though, all bets are off!

When I'm not in class or studying, I'm usually missing my husband and pets (1 dog, 3 cats), who are still in the 'Burgh. I might also be watching a Steelers game, if it's football season. Or drinking some sort of Belgian beer - tripel, quad, dubbel ... I'm open-minded. Sadly, Seattle is Land of the IPA.

When I'm in Pittsburgh, my home office is also my craft room, and I might be in there sewing, making party invitations, or trying something random I saw on Pinterest.

In my perfect world, I would be surrounded by animals. All the time.
